Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are one of the most popular dog breeds. Known for their friendly, intelligent, and devoted nature, they make excellent family pets and service dogs. Originally bred in Scotland during the 19th century, they were designed to retrieve game during hunting expeditions. Their beautiful golden coat and loving temperament have made them beloved worldwide.
